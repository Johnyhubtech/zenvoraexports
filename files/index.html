Who We Are

Connecting Africa's finest agricultural harvests to the world's most discerning markets.

Zenvora Global Industries Limited is a premier agricultural export company headquartered in Bida, Niger State — at the fertile heart of Nigeria. We specialise in sourcing, processing, and exporting the finest natural commodities, carrying the richness of Africa's soil to global markets.

Incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 and registered with the Nigerian Export Promotion Council (NEPC), we operate with full regulatory compliance, uncompromising quality standards, and deep commitment to the farming communities that make our work possible.

Responsible Sourcing

Quality and responsibility, the same standard

Our supply chain runs through real communities. Treating farmers fairly and managing land responsibly isn't separate from export quality — it's the foundation of it.

"Premium export quality and responsible sourcing are not a trade-off — at Zenvora, they are the same standard."

Ethical Sourcing

Every farmer cluster we work with is identified, registered and paid fairly and promptly for produce delivered.

Farmer Empowerment

We provide access to improved post-harvest handling practices, helping farmers raise yield quality and reduce losses.

Fair Trade Practices

Pricing is transparent and benchmarked to international markets — not dictated by layers of intermediaries.

Community Development

A share of export proceeds supports local storage infrastructure and seasonal credit access for farming communities.

Sustainable Agriculture

We encourage crop rotation, responsible input use and land-management practices across our sourcing network.

Leadership

A message from our founder

"We don't just export commodities — we export the pride, the labour, and the extraordinary richness of Nigeria's land to the world."

Engr. Kingsley Okoro
Founder & Chief Executive Officer

At Zenvora Global Industries Limited, we do not merely trade commodities — we deliver trust, quality, and the extraordinary natural abundance that Nigeria's land has to offer to the world. Nigeria is home to some of the world's most prized agricultural commodities, yet for far too long, our farmers and our nation have not received the full value their harvests deserve. Zenvora was founded with a clear and purposeful mission: to bridge that gap — connecting the hard work of Nigerian farming communities directly to international markets that recognise and reward quality. As an engineer by training, I bring precision, systems thinking, and rigorous quality standards to everything we do.
